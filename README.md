# Kitfox

A manuscript and continuity archive for **Jane the Pilot**.

Jane is a young pilot with a black modified Kitfox, a dead pair of brilliant guardians, and a dangerous amount of real competence without the permissions that are supposed to accompany it. The story begins when he arrives on **St. Jude’s Head**, a remote Alaskan island whose weather, cliffs, rescue culture, and local pride make room for him before anyone is willing to admit they have done so.

## Core continuity

- Jane is sixteen.
- He flies a black modified Kitfox.
- He hates shoes and avoids them whenever possible.
- He was raised by two cold, brilliant, loving foster guardians: Mara, a mathematician, and Elias, a doctor.
- After Mara died, Elias brought Jane overseas on medical volunteer trips, where Jane gained serious practical medical experience.
- Elias died shortly before Jane arrived on St. Jude’s Head.
- St. Jude’s Head is a remote Alaskan island in the United States.
- The island has an official standard featuring a severe, oddly militant old-style St. Jude figure. Locals are proud of it. SAR displays it often. Most local pilots put it on their aircraft. Jane does not — not for a while.
